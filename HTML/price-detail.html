For children aged up to 3 years admission is free of charge.

Concessionary tickets eligibility: pre-school children, primary and secondary school students aged up to 19 years, undergraduate and doctoral university students, old age and disability pensioners, as well as disabled people with their carers, upon presentation of relevant documents.

Group tickets eligibility: groups of 10 people or more. As for organised groups, 1 group organiser per each 10 people is entitled to admission free of charge.

Family tickets with a Big Family Card eligibility: 2 adult people with at least 3 children. Holders of a Big Family Card and "Two plus three and more" card. Also purchase of single tickets (regular or concessionary) is possible, with an additional discount for holders of a Big Family Card. Eligibility for purchase of tickets with a Big Family Card is checked upon presentation of relevant documents at Aquvapolis ticket office before entry to the exhibition.
